RPME — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

The short version: RPME does not collect, transmit, or sell your data. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising. Everything you enter stays on your own device.

What we collect

Nothing. RPME has no servers, no user accounts, and no sign-in. We do not receive, see, or store any information about you.

What the app stores on your device

RPME saves the data you enter locally on your device (in browser/app local storage) so your progress persists between sessions. This may include:

  • Body measurements, bodyweight, and progress photos you choose to add
  • Workout logs — exercises, sets, reps, and weights
  • Daily habit check-offs and personal notes
  • Training goals, schedule, and in-app preferences

This information never leaves your device unless you deliberately export it yourself. Progress photos are stored locally and are never uploaded.

Third-party requests

We want to be precise rather than claim more than is true. The app loads two external resources when you have an internet connection:

  • Chart.js from cdnjs.cloudflare.com — used to draw progress charts
  • Google Fonts from fonts.googleapis.com — used for typography

These are code and font files only. None of your personal or fitness data is included in these requests. However, as with loading any file from the internet, the provider receives your device's IP address and standard request headers. Their handling of that is governed by their own policies: Cloudflare and Google.

The app makes no other network requests of any kind. It contains no analytics, telemetry, tracking pixels, advertising, or crash reporting.

Exporting and deleting your data

You are in full control:

  • Export — the in-app backup creates a file saved directly to your device. It is not sent anywhere.
  • Delete — clearing the app's storage (or uninstalling the app) permanently erases all of your data.

Because we never receive your data, we cannot recover it for you. Please keep your own backups.

Children

RPME is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone — including children — because we do not collect information at all.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised date above. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.
